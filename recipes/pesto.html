pesto delicioso

Description

Pesto is great and I like it. You make it with basil, oil, parmesan cheese and sometimes pine nuts if you're fancy.

You can put it on anything, including cake, your bathtub, even your friends if they aren't looking.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup pine nuts
  • 4 cloves garlic, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice, or more to taste (optional)

    • Directions

    Vigorously mix all the ingredients together until it looks ready to toss at someone.
